Privacy Policy

Effective May 24, 2026
Happy Frog Studio ("we", "us", "our") takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through the Velora app (the "App"), how we handle it, and what rights you have.

1Information We Process

Workout Photos

What
Photos you add to your workout entries
How collected
You select or capture photos directly within the App
Purpose
Visualize workouts and display them on the calendar
Handling
Stored only on your device. Not transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Workout Data (steps, distance, duration, calories, etc.)

What
Activity data read from Apple Health or Samsung Health
How collected
Automatically read via Apple HealthKit / Samsung Health SDK after you grant permission
Purpose
Auto-collect workout records, display statistics, calculate goal progress
Handling
Stored only on your device. Not transmitted to our servers or any third party. We only read from Apple Health / Samsung Health. We never write data back.

Advertising Identifier

What
Advertising ID (Apple IDFA / Google Advertising ID)
How collected
Automatically collected via Google AdMob SDK
Purpose
Ad delivery and measurement within the App
Reference
We do not collect this directly. Google AdMob's Privacy Policy applies.

2Information We Do Not Collect

  • User accounts (Velora does not require sign-up)
  • Personal identifiers such as email, phone number, or name
  • In-app behavior analytics
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos outside what you choose, or any data requiring additional permissions

3Data Retention and Deletion

  • All workout photos and data remain on your device. We do not store them separately.
  • Uninstalling the App removes all data automatically (no separate process required).
  • You can delete individual workout entries or photos anytime through the App.
  • Advertising identifiers are processed and deleted according to Google AdMob's policies (see Section 1.3 reference link).

4Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any personal information with third parties. Advertising identifiers are processed directly by Google AdMob as described in Section 1.3.

5Data Processors

We engage the following service provider to operate the App. The processor handles data solely within the scope of the entrusted purpose.

Processor
Google LLC (AdMob)
Service
In-app ad delivery and measurement
Data handled
Advertising Identifier (Apple IDFA / Google Advertising ID)

6Your Rights and How to Exercise Them

You have the following rights and can exercise them at any time.

  • Access, Correction, Deletion: All workout records and photos are stored on your device, so you can view, modify, and delete them directly through the App.
  • Withdraw Permissions: You can revoke Apple Health / Samsung Health permissions anytime through your device settings.
  • Limit Ad Tracking: You can limit ad tracking through your iOS / Android settings.
  • Bulk Deletion: Uninstalling the App deletes all data stored on your device.
  • To exercise these rights or for inquiries, contact the Data Protection Officer below.

7Automatically Collected Information

This App does not use cookies or web tracking technologies. The Advertising Identifier described in Section 1.3 is automatically collected and processed by the Google AdMob SDK.

8Children's Privacy

This App is not directed to children under 14, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14. If a child under 14 uses this App, parental or legal guardian consent is required. Any information collected without proper consent will be deleted immediately upon discovery.

9Security Measures

  • All personal information is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • We do not operate a separate personal information database, eliminating the risk of external breaches at the source.
  • Advertising identifiers are handled according to Google AdMob's security policies.
